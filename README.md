## 🚀 About Me 
I’m a Lead Data & Analytics Engineer with 10+ years of experience designing and delivering scalable, cloud-native data and analytics platforms across AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud Platform (GCP). I specialize in building end-to-end data solutions that transform raw data into trusted, actionable insights, enabling data-driven decision-making and sustainable business growth.

I bring deep expertise across data engineering, analytics, and platform architecture, with a strong focus on delivering high-impact solutions across Supply Chain, Financial, Operational, Sales & Marketing, Customer & Product Analytics, Demand Forecasting, Inventory Optimization, Procurement, Manufacturing, and Enterprise IT analytics.

My core strengths include designing robust ETL/ELT pipelines, implementing modern data warehousing solutions, and developing enterprise-grade BI dashboards using Power BI, Tableau, and Splunk. I have hands-on experience building scalable data pipelines with Apache NiFi, Airflow, AWS Glue, Azure Data Factory, Databricks, and Informatica, along with expertise in big data and real-time processing using Spark, Kafka, Hadoop, Hive, and Scala.

I am highly skilled in data modeling and performance optimization, specializing in dimensional modeling (star and snowflake schemas) and advanced SQL tuning. I have architected and optimized cloud data platforms using Snowflake, Amazon Redshift, and Azure Synapse Analytics, ensuring high performance, scalability, and reliability.

In addition, I drive DataOps and automation practices, implementing CI/CD pipelines and orchestration using Jenkins, GitLab CI, Docker, and Kubernetes to streamline data workflows and improve delivery efficiency.

🚀 Leadership & Impact

Throughout my career, I have led enterprise-scale data transformation initiatives, including:
Modernizing legacy data platforms into cloud-native architectures
Migrating on-premises systems to multi-cloud environments
Optimizing ETL/ELT workflows for performance and scalability

I focus on building data products that deliver measurable business value — improving data accessibility, governance, and decision-making across organizations. I also actively mentor teams, define data strategy, and establish best practices in data architecture and analytics engineering.
